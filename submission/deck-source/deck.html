The Problem: Fashion E Commerce is Broken

Myntra serves 50M+ monthly active users3 across India, offering 9 lakh+ products from 5,000+ brands. Despite this scale, the platform leaks money through fundamental UX inefficiencies that cost over ₹2,000 Cr a year in returns alone. The root cause is structural: users navigate an enormous catalogue through keyword search and static filters, tools built for the desktop era, not for how people actually think about fashion.
70%1
Cart Abandonment
Users add items but cannot decide. Choice overload creates decision paralysis. The average shopper browses 40+ products before a single purchase.
35%2
Return Rate
Fashion has the highest return rate in e commerce. The primary driver is fit and style mismatch, because shoppers cannot try on and size charts are unreliable.
3.2 min1
Time to Find
Average time to surface one relevant product. Users scroll past pages of irrelevant results because search reads keywords, not intent or context.
₹2,100 Cr4
Annual Return Cost
Reverse logistics, restocking, customer service and damaged goods. This is pure cost that generates zero revenue and scales directly with GMV.
Who is affected? Four user segments
The Browser (62% of DAU)
Scrolls endlessly, rarely converts. Wants curated discovery that does the editing for them.
The Returner (31% return rate)
Buys five, keeps one. Fit and style mismatch is the core pain. Needs trustworthy fit intelligence.
The Occasion Shopper (high AOV)
Needs a complete outfit for a wedding, party or fest, with no idea where to start. Needs intent based curation.
The Trend Chaser (age 18 to 28)
Wants celebrity and influencer looks at their own budget. Needs visual search and trend matching.
Hypothesis: a Generative AI styling layer can reduce returns by 25%, lift conversion by 18% and raise AOV by 22%, creating ₹1,200 to 1,700 Cr in annual impact without re platforming the app.

Why Generative AI? Traditional Approaches Have Hit a Ceiling

Myntra already runs predictive ML such as collaborative filtering and content based recommendations. These handle simple cases but fundamentally cannot solve the problems above, because they predict from the past rather than understand the present request. The table below pairs a real user need with the traditional approach, why it breaks, and the categorically new capability GenAI unlocks.

My Approach and the StyleGenie Solution

My approach: from problem to solution
01DIAGNOSE
Studied four root pains: choice overload, fit uncertainty, context blindness and a manual discovery loop.
02REFRAME
Shifted from inventory first ("browse 9 lakh products") to intent first ("look great at a wedding under ₹5K").
03DESIGN
Mapped each segment and pain to one specific GenAI capability, so every feature solves a real need with no overlap.
04VALIDATE
Anchored every feature to a measurable KPI: return rate, conversion, AOV and DAU. Nothing ships without a metric.
The solution: six GenAI features (five core, plus one signature)
💬 StyleGenie Chat
Solves choice overload
A conversational stylist that reads occasion, budget and style from plain language. LLM plus RAG: intent parse, vector search, compose, stream. Cuts 40 products to 5 looks.
📷 Visual Match
Solves style discovery
Upload a photo or screenshot and find visually similar Myntra products. CLIP encodes the image, cosine similarity ranks the catalogue. 35% CTR.
🪄 Outfit Studio
Solves no outfit thinking
Pick one product and get 3 to 5 complete styled looks from real items. Colour theory plus occasion rules. Adds 1.8 items per order.
🎯 FitGenius
Solves fit uncertainty
Predicts your size with 90%+ confidence using review mining and a body profile. Evidence is shown. Returns fall from 32% to under 18%.
📈 TrendPulse
Solves static discovery
A personalised daily feed of AI written trend lookbooks. Social signal to narrative to style graph match. Drives a 40% DAU lift.
🧳 Capsule ✦ SIGNATURE
Solves value and basket size
Give a budget and the occasions you dress for; StyleGenie builds a tiny set of mix and match pieces and shows how many complete outfits they unlock. Generative composition as budget optimisation.
Segment to feature:  Browser to Chat plus TrendPulse  ·  Returner to FitGenius plus Studio  ·  Occasion to Chat plus Capsule  ·  Trend Chaser to Visual plus TrendPulse

Risks, Pitfalls and Mitigation Strategy

Every AI product has failure modes. The discipline is to design for them upfront, not to discover them in production. These are the six most critical risks for StyleGenie, each rated by severity with a specific, implementable mitigation.
⚠️ HallucinationsCritical
RISK
AI suggests products that do not exist, are out of stock, or sit in the wrong category, which destroys trust instantly.
MITIGATION
Every response is grounded through a RAG pipeline,1 so the LLM never invents product data and only references the live catalogue. Confidence scoring filters low certainty output, and every card links to a real PDP.
🛡️ PrivacyHigh
RISK
Users are uncomfortable sharing body measurements, photos or style data, alongside India DPDP Act obligations.
MITIGATION
On device image processing, explicit opt in per data type, zero third party sharing, and full DPDP Act compliance2 with deletion on request.
⚡ LatencyHigh
RISK
LLM inference takes 3 to 5 seconds, far too slow for a shopping flow where users expect near instant results.
MITIGATION
Streaming responses to cut perceived wait, pre computed outfit caches for common queries, and an edge deployed CLIP model. Target service level under 2 seconds for all AI features.
🤝 User TrustMedium
RISK
"How can a machine know what looks good on me?" Adoption depends on trust in the advice.
MITIGATION
Transparent confidence scores, evidence based explanations ("based on 10,247 reviews"), and a manual override at every step. The AI earns credibility through accuracy over time.
📈 ScalabilityHigh
RISK
GPT-4o costs at Myntra scale (50M+ MAU) could reach ₹50 to 100 Cr a month, unsustainable without optimisation.
MITIGATION
Tiered models (Gemini Flash or Haiku for simple queries, premium only for complex styling), aggressive caching so similar queries share one response, and batch processing for TrendPulse.
👥 AdoptionMedium
RISK
Users default to the familiar browse, filter and scroll loop, and AI features get ignored if they feel like a separate experience.3
MITIGATION
Soft inline integration, so AI appears within the existing flow, not a separate tab. Contextual nudges on struggle signals, plus a free tier to build the habit and a premium upsell later.
References: 1 Lewis et al., Retrieval Augmented Generation, 2020 · 2 India DPDP Act, 2023 · 3 Nielsen Norman Group, AI Feature Adoption Patterns
